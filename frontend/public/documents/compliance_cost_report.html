Methodology & Notes

¹ Bills evaluated = bills with complete compliance milestones; bills still in progress with incomplete records were excluded. “Violations” = bills classified Non-Compliant (missed one or more statutory requirements).

² Chair Stipend = the Section 9B stipend for one committee chair position (M.G.L. c.3 §9B). Legislators who chair multiple committees receive this amount per qualifying position, subject to a two-position cap under Senate Rule 11E.

³ Total Pay = base salary + all Section 9B stipends + travel allowance (M.G.L. c.3 §§9B–9C). Joint committee chairs appear once per chamber.
